Father of\
Here's a **clean, exam-focused list of "Fathers of" in Computer
Science** 👇

## 💻 Core Computer Science

- **Father of Computer** → Charles Babbage
- **Father of Modern Computer Science** → Alan Turing
- **Father of Personal Computer (PC)** → Ed Roberts
- **Father of Supercomputer** → Seymour Cray

## 🌐 Internet & Web

- **Father of the Internet** → Vint Cerf (also Bob Kahn)
- **Father of the World Wide Web** → Tim Berners-Lee

## 🖥️ Programming & Software

- **Father of C Language** → Dennis Ritchie
- **Father of C++** → Bjarne Stroustrup
- **Father of Java** → James Gosling
- **Father of Python** → Guido van Rossum

## 🧠 Artificial Intelligence & Computing Concepts

- **Father of Artificial Intelligence** → John McCarthy
- **Father of Information Theory** → Claude Shannon
- **Father of Algorithm Analysis** → Donald Knuth

## 🗄️ Databases & Systems

- **Father of Database** → Edgar F. Codd
- **Father of UNIX** → Ken Thompson (also Dennis Ritchie)

## 🧾 Extras (Often Asked in Exams)

- **First Programmer** → Ada Lovelace
- **Father of Email** → Ray Tomlinson

## ⚡ Quick Memory Trick

- **Babbage → Computer**
- **Turing → Theory**
- **Cerf → Internet**
- **Berners-Lee → Web**
- **Ritchie → C**
- **McCarthy → AI**
