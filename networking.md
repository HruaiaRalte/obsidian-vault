599\. Appropriate transmission medium carrying data exposed to
electrical interfaces: (a) Unshielded twisted pair (b) Optical fiber (c)
Coaxial cable (d) Microwave

The correct answer is:

**(a) Unshielded twisted pair**

### Explanation:

**Unshielded Twisted Pair (UTP)** cables:

- Carry data using **electrical signals**
- Are commonly used in **LAN networks (Ethernet cables)**
- Are **exposed to electrical interference** because they lack shielding

Other options:

- Optical fiber → uses light, not electrical signals ❌
- Coaxial cable → has shielding, less exposed ❌
- Microwave → wireless transmission ❌

✅ So, **UTP** is correct.
