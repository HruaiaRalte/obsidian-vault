# 1. DEFINITION

**Digital Divide** means:

> The gap between people who **have access to digital technology and the
> internet** and those who **do not**.

Technology includes:

- computers
- smartphones
- internet
- digital services

Example

    Urban area → fast internet
    Rural area → little or no internet

This difference is **Digital Divide**.

# 2. TYPES OF DIGITAL DIVIDE

    Digital Divide
    │
    ├─ Access Divide
    ├─ Usage Divide
    └─ Quality Divide

### 1. Access Divide

Difference in **physical access to technology**.

Example:

- No internet
- No computer

### 2. Usage Divide

Difference in **ability to use technology**.

Example:

- Some people know computers
- Some people do not

### 3. Quality Divide

Difference in **speed and quality of internet**.

Example:

    Country A → fiber internet
    Country B → slow mobile network

# 3. CAUSES OF DIGITAL DIVIDE

    Causes

# 4. EFFECTS OF DIGITAL DIVIDE

    ├─ Limited job opportunities
